# Hack Jack London Square — Nebius Builders Boat Hackathon

A one-day Oakland hackathon staged across Jack London Square partners, anchored by the **Dragon Lady** boat. Builders cross the bay from South Beach in the morning, hack at Plank, take a sunset networking cruise with dinner on board, and after-party at Heinold's.

**Status:** planning
**Headcount cap:** 50 (Dragon Lady max)
**Target date:** Saturday or Sunday — May 30 is a candidate but may carry premium pricing
**Working budget:** ~$5k for Plank half-day takeover; sponsor-funded
**Operator:** AgentHack (also runs events at Shack15, Frontier Tower, Founders Inc.)
**Contact email:** events@agenthack.ai

---

## Concept

The Jack London Oakland experience as a hackathon. Every venue is a Jack London Square business — builders move on foot between Plank, Bicycle Coffee, the Dragon Lady dock, and Heinold's. The boat ride is the wedge: networking time on the water, sunset cruise with dinner, and a built-in cap on group size.

Working name: **Hack Jack London Square** (a.k.a. "Hack Jack").
